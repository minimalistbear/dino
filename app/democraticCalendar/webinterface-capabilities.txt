Webinterface Capabilities

2-Tabbed Site:
	Make Appointment
	Query Appointment

Make Appointment
1. Connect my Wallet
   (interface with MetaMask and display wallet ID, status: connected)
2. Enter Personal Information
   and create individual hash value
3. Enter Date: Year, Month, Day
   and display free & taken time slots
4. Select Free Slot
5. Make Appointment

Query Appointment
1. Connect my Wallet
2. Enter Personal Information
3. Show Appointment
